# Privacy Policy for QR & Barcode Scanner

**Last updated:** May 2026

Yagiz K built the QR & Barcode Scanner app as a Free app. This SERVICE is provided by Yagiz K at no cost and is intended for use as is.

## Information Collection and Use
This application does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. All generated and scanned QR codes/barcodes are processed and stored locally on your device.

## Camera Permission
The app requires access to your device's camera strictly for the purpose of scanning QR codes and barcodes in real-time. We do not take photos, record videos, or upload any camera feed to external servers.

## Third-Party Services (AdMob)
The app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain data (such as your device's Advertising ID) to provide personalized ads.
